# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Contributors are expected to communicate directly, respectfully, and in good faith.

Examples of positive behavior:

- discussing technical disagreements with evidence
- keeping review feedback specific and actionable
- respecting time and attention in issues and pull requests
- focusing criticism on code and design, not people

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- personal attacks
- harassment
- discriminatory language
- deliberate disruption of collaboration

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or restrict participation when behavior makes collaboration unsafe or unproductive.
